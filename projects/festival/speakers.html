Kraków, Polna 17
EXPLORER
Academic IT Festival

Speakers

The IT space is vast and there is much to explore. There are a great many topics that may be discussed and the lectures you find here can range from broad subjects that encompass the whole IT sector to specialist topics that could prove inspirational when choosing your career path. Our speakers are often specialists and have much to share about their journey to how they became as accomplished as they are. They want to share what they know to those who are interested simply because they are passionate about the topic.

The final topic breakdown of the conference will be determined after the Call for Papers, but will be analogous to that of previous editions of the event.

Do you have an idea for an interesting topic and would like to be a speaker at our event? If so, please fill out the form on the Get Involved page.
