Academic IT Festival

About

Explorer is a festival organized by students from Kraków universities for everyone interested in the field of Information Technology. Our goal is to create a space for IT-enthusiasts from different fields and with different levels of experience, where everybody can find something interesting.

Originally Explorer was a small festival that only hosted local IT businesses from Kraków and nearby cities. Over the years it has expanded and now boasts interest from both organisations and students from across the globe. Explorer is now recognised as a fantastic place to learn, network and share everything new in the IT universe. Due to its growth, Explorer is now hosted in English to appeal to the international community.

The festival consists of lectures, workshops and discussion panels, hosting specialists from the entire IT environment. It's a great opportunity to discover and explore new areas of the IT world and expand your horizons. The diversity of the topics as well as difficulty levels allow everybody to find something for themselves.

And the best part is – it's for free!

That's right, Explorer is entirely for free thanks to our sponsors. Sponsors as well as job fair exhibitors have a unique opportunity to increase their recognition among people in the IT field and reach young people available in the job market. If you are an undergraduate or recently graduated, then this is a fantastic gateway into an IT career.

Come join us at Explorer and explore the unknown with us!


Organizers

Historically Explorer has been organised by the second and third year students studying in the IT and engineering departments of Kraków Universities. With some assistance from their sponsors, they organise everything from the time of the activities to the constant supply of coffee and our iconic 'Full Snack Developer' snacks to all participants. Here is a list of student study groups and organizations involved with the Explorer:

Student Society Hip Hackers from Kraków University of Technology

Student Organization Binary from Kraków University of Science and Technology

Student Society Fraktal from Warsaw University of Technology

Organization Jeden, Zero, Go! from Kraków University of Economics

Student Society CodeFun from Katowice University of Technology

Study Group Lady Bug from School of Computer Science and Economics of Kraków
