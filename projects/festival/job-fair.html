Job Fair

One of Explorer’s most exciting events is the job fair. Companies from across the globe, both international and regional, come in search for talented students and graduates. The opportunities they bring include full time jobs, apprenticeships, year in industry for undergraduates and sometimes summer work opportunities abroad. It’s a wonderful chance for students to find different job roles in the IT industry and for networking in advance of graduation. The job types can range dramatically including programming robotic arms, hacking cryptocurrencies and game design to name just a few!

The Job fair is also a good opportunity for those in already established careers to network and find other possibilities of work that may be more suited to them. Explorer encourages everyone to keep pushing the boundaries of what is known, why should it be restricted to a job role?

The Job fair each year continues to grow bigger with more companies looking to advertise themselves. Although we account for this, Explorer still often runs out of spaces for exhibits. If you would like your company to be present at the job fair then you can apply on the Get involved page.
