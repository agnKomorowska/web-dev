Sponsors

Since 2005 Explorer festival opened itself up to sponsorships and has since has gained notable fame and influence in the IT world. With the funding from our sponsors, Explorer has gone from a 1 day small time event to a 3 day festival renowned worldwide for it’s informative, entertaining and engaging approach to sharing and networking in the IT space.

Special thanks go to our primary sponsor SUBIRO, whom without, Explorer would not have been able to accomplish what it has done today.

Would you like to support Explorer and make your organization more recognizable among the best specialists in the IT world? If so, please contact us using the form on the Get Involved page.

Gold Sponsor

Subiro Poland

Subiro is one of the most innovative software solution providers and a pioneer in the world of outsourcing. With over 15 years in the business and 8 offices in Europe, we provide a diverse range of software development and support solutions to various businesses around the world. We assist businesses and organizations in creating exciting and high quality products in stable environments. Subiro has also been ranked as one of the top junior-friendly IT companies, embracing the power of knowledge sharing and colaboration.
